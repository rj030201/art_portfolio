biography

Rebecca Huteson is a multi-media artist living in Dallas, Texas. She graduated from Southwestern University in 2017 where she recieved a B.A. in environmental studies with a minor in studio art. Her media concentration in college was oil painting, a medium she still explores today. She has maintained a consistent art practice since graduation, expanding her media repertoire. She spent time teaching art at a private elementary school, an experience that, she says, helped to widen her understanding of arts impact in the world.

Rebecca currently works fulltime as a prompt engineer for a company that helps to train artificial intelligence models and practices art in her free time. Her current media of choice is graphite and she's in the process of completing new work. Stay tuned!
